Software Engineering Student & Tech Leader

I'm a Computer Software Engineering student at Pan-Atlantic University with hands-on experience in full-stack development, UI/UX design, and applied AI. I'm passionate about building software that solves real-world problems while maintaining the highest standards of security and ethics.

My journey in technology is driven by a deep commitment to creating solutions that genuinely improve people's lives. Whether it's developing enterprise inventory systems that streamline operations, designing intuitive user interfaces that enhance digital experiences, or exploring machine learning applications, I approach every project with the same core principle: technology should serve humanity, not the other way around.

As Vice President of the Tech Innovation Club at Pan-Atlantic University, I lead strategic initiatives and technical projects while mentoring fellow students. This role has taught me the importance of collaborative leadership, effective communication, and the ability to bridge the gap between technical execution and strategic vision. I believe that the best solutions emerge when diverse perspectives come together with a shared purpose.

My experience spans from enterprise web development during my internship at Swap Technologies, where I built custom inventory management systems for EV conversion kits, to Python development at CodSoft, where I honed my coding practices in agile environments. Each experience has reinforced my belief that great software is built at the intersection of technical excellence, user empathy, and ethical consideration.

Beyond coding, I'm deeply invested in the broader tech community. As a Campus Ambassador for Cowrywise, I facilitate financial literacy initiatives, recognizing that technology's impact extends beyond code into economic empowerment. My involvement in PAU Women in STEM and various committee roles reflects my commitment to creating inclusive spaces where everyone can thrive in technology.

I'm constantly learning and evolving, currently advancing my expertise in data structures, software design patterns, and system architecture. My goal is to become a well-rounded software engineer who can design and build scalable, secure systems while never losing sight of the human element at the heart of all technology.
